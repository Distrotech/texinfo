4.10 module

Modules are submodules of a free module over the basering with basis gen(1), gen(2), … . They are represented by lists of vectors which generate the submodule. Like vectors they can only be defined or accessed with respect to a basering. If is a submodule of ,

the basering, generated by vectors , then may be considered as the generators of relations of between the canonical generators gen(1),…,gen(n). Hence any finitely generated -module can be represented in SINGULAR by its module of relations.
