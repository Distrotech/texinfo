D.5.4 gaussman_lib

Library:

gaussman.lib

Purpose:

Algorithmic Gauss-Manin Connection

Author:

Mathias Schulze, email: mschulze@mathematik.uni-kl.de

Overview:

A library to compute Hodge-theoretic invariants
of isolated hypersurface singularities

Procedures:

D.5.4.1 gmsring   Gauss-Manin system of t with variable s
D.5.4.2 gmsnf   Gauss-Manin normal form of p
D.5.4.3 gmscoeffs   Gauss-Manin basis representation of p
D.5.4.4 bernstein   roots of the Bernstein polynomial of t
D.5.4.5 monodromy   Jordan data of complex monodromy of t
D.5.4.6 spectrum   singularity spectrum of t
D.5.4.7 sppairs   spectral pairs of t
D.5.4.8 spnf   spectrum normal form of (a,m,V)
D.5.4.9 sppnf   spectral pairs normal form of (a,w,m,V)
D.5.4.10 vfilt   V-filtration of t on Brieskorn lattice
D.5.4.11 vwfilt   weighted V-filtration of t on Brieskorn lattice
D.5.4.12 tmatrix   C[[s]]-matrix of t on Brieskorn lattice
D.5.4.13 endvfilt   endomorphism V-filtration on Jacobian algebra
D.5.4.14 spprint   print spectrum sp
D.5.4.15 sppprint   print spectral pairs spp
D.5.4.16 spadd   sum of spectra sp1 and sp2
D.5.4.17 spsub   difference of spectra sp1 and sp2
D.5.4.18 spmul   linear combination of spectra sp
D.5.4.19 spissemicont   semicontinuity test of spectrum sp
D.5.4.20 spsemicont   semicontinuous combinations of spectra sp0 in sp
D.5.4.21 spmilnor   Milnor number of spectrum sp
D.5.4.22 spgeomgenus   geometrical genus of spectrum sp
D.5.4.23 spgamma   gamma invariant of spectrum sp

See also: mondromy_lib; spectrum_lib.
