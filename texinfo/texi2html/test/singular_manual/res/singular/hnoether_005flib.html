D.5.5 hnoether_lib

Library:

hnoether.lib

Purpose:

Hamburger-Noether (Puiseux) Development

Author:

Martin Lamm, lamm@mathematik.uni-kl.de

Overview:

A library for computing the Hamburger-Noether, resp. Puiseux, development of a plane curve singularity following [Campillo, A.: Algebroid curves in positive characteristic, Springer LNM 813 (1980)].
The library contains also procedures for computing the (topological) numerical invariants of plane curve singularities.

Main procedures:

D.5.5.1 hnexpansion   Hamburger-Noether (H-N) development of f
D.5.5.2 sethnering   changes to the hnering created by hnexpansion
D.5.5.3 develop   H-N development of irreducible curves
D.5.5.4 extdevelop   extension of the H-N development hne of f
D.5.5.5 parametrisation   a parametrization of f
D.5.5.6 displayHNE   display H-N development as an ideal
D.5.5.7 invariants   invariants of f, e.g. the characteristic exponents
D.5.5.8 displayInvariants   display invariants of f
D.5.5.9 multsequence   sequence of multiplicities
D.5.5.10 displayMultsequence   display sequence of multiplicities
D.5.5.11 intersection   intersection multiplicity of two curves
D.5.5.12 stripHNE   reduce amount of memory consumed by hne
D.5.5.13 is_irred   test if f is irreducible
D.5.5.14 delta   delta invariant of f
D.5.5.15 newtonpoly   (local) Newton polygon of f
D.5.5.16 is_NND   test if f is Newton non-degenerate

Auxiliary procedures:

D.5.5.17 puiseux2generators   convert Puiseux pairs to generators of semigroup
D.5.5.18 separateHNE   number of quadratic transf. needed for separation
D.5.5.19 squarefree   a squarefree divisor of the poly f
D.5.5.20 allsquarefree   the maximal squarefree divisor of the poly f
D.5.5.21 further_hn_proc   show further procedures useful for interactive use
