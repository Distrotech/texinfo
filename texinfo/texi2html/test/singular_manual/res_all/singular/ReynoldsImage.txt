D.6.3.12 ReynoldsImage
......................
Procedure from library rinvar.lib (see rinvar_lib).

Usage:
ReynoldsImage(RO, f); list RO, poly f

Purpose:
compute the Reynolds image of the polynomial f where RO represents
the Reynolds operator

Return:
poly
