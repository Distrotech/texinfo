D.5.8.11 slocus
...............
Procedure from library sing.lib (see sing_lib).

Usage:
slocus(i); i ideal

Return:
ideal of singular locus of i

Example:
LIB "sing.lib";
ring r  = 0,(u,v,w,x,y,z),dp;
ideal i = wx,wy,wz,vx,vy,vz,ux,uy,uz,y3-x2;;
slocus(i);
==> _[1]=x
==> _[2]=w
==> _[3]=v
==> _[4]=u
==> _[5]=y2
