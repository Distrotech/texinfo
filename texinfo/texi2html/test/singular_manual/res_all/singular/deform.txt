D.5.8.1 deform
..............
Procedure from library sing.lib (see sing_lib).

Usage:
deform(id); id=ideal or poly

Return:
matrix, columns are kbase of infinitesimal deformations

Example:
LIB "sing.lib";
ring r   = 32003,(x,y,z),ds;
ideal i  = xy,xz,yz;
matrix T = deform(i);
print(T);
==> x,0,0,
==> 0,0,z,
==> 0,y,0 
print(deform(x3+y5+z2));
==> xy3,y3,xy2,y2,xy,y,x,1
