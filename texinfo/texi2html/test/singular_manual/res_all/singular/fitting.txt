D.4.3.6 fitting
...............
Procedure from library homolog.lib (see homolog_lib).

Usage:
fitting (M,n); M module, n int

Return:
ideal, (standard basis of) n-th Fitting ideal of M'=coker(M).

Example:
LIB "homolog.lib";
ring R=0,x(0..4),dp;
matrix M[2][4]=x(0),x(1),x(2),x(3),x(1),x(2),x(3),x(4);
print(M);
==> x(0),x(1),x(2),x(3),
==> x(1),x(2),x(3),x(4) 
fitting(M,-1);
==> _[1]=0
fitting(M,0);
==> _[1]=x(3)^2-x(2)*x(4)
==> _[2]=x(2)*x(3)-x(1)*x(4)
==> _[3]=x(1)*x(3)-x(0)*x(4)
==> _[4]=x(2)^2-x(0)*x(4)
==> _[5]=x(1)*x(2)-x(0)*x(3)
==> _[6]=x(1)^2-x(0)*x(2)
fitting(M,1);
==> _[1]=x(4)
==> _[2]=x(3)
==> _[3]=x(2)
==> _[4]=x(1)
==> _[5]=x(0)
fitting(M,2);
==> _[1]=1
