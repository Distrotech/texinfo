4.6 link
========

Links are the communication channels of SINGULAR, i.e.,
something SINGULAR can write to and/or read from. Currently,
SINGULAR supports four different link types:
* ASCII links (see
ASCII links)

* MPfile links (see
MPfile links)

* MPtcp links (see
