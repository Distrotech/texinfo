D.5.1.13 normalform
...................
Procedure from library classify.lib (see classify_lib).

Usage:
normalform(s); s=string

Return:
Arnold's normal form of singularity with name s

Example:
LIB "classify.lib";
ring r=0,(a,b,c),ds;
normalform("E[13]");
==> c2+a3+ab5+b8
