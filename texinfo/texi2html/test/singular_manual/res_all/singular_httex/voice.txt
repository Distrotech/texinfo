5.3.11 voice
------------

Type:
int
Purpose:
shows the nesting level of procedures.
Note:
See Guidelines for writing a library, for an small example how this is used
for the display of comments while procedures are executed.
Example:
  voice;
==> 1
proc p
{
  voice;
};
p();
==> 2
See
dbprint;
listvar;
printlevel.
