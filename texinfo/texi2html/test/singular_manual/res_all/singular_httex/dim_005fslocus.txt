D.5.8.2 dim_slocus
..................
Procedure from library sing.lib (see sing_lib).

Usage:
dim_slocus(i); i ideal or poly

Return:
dimension of singular locus of i

Example:
LIB "sing.lib";
ring r  = 32003,(x,y,z),ds;
ideal i = x5+y6+z6,x2+2y2+3z2;
dim_slocus(i);
==> 0
