5.1.96 prime
------------
Syntax:
prime ( int_expression )
Type:
int
Purpose:
returns the largest prime less then 32004 smaller or equal to the argument;

returns 2 for all arguments smaller than 3.
Example:
  prime(32004);
==> 32003
  prime(0);
==> 2
  prime(-1);
==> 2
See
general_lib;
int.
