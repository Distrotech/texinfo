D.5.4.21 spmilnor
.................
Procedure from library gaussman.lib (see gaussman_lib).

Usage:
spmilnor(sp); list sp

Return:
int mu; Milnor number of spectrum sp

Example:
LIB "gaussman.lib";
ring R=0,(x,y),ds;
list sp=list(ideal(-1/2,-3/10,-1/10,0,1/10,3/10,1/2),intvec(1,2,2,1,2,2,1));
spprint(sp);
==> (-1/2,1),(-3/10,2),(-1/10,2),(0,1),(1/10,2),(3/10,2),(1/2,1)
spmilnor(sp);
==> 11
