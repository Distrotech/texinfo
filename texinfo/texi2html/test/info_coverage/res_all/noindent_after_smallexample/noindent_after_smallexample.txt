You can obtain the newest version of `config.guess' and
`config.sub' from the CVS of the `config' project at
`http://savannah.gnu.org/'. The commands to fetch them are
$ wget 'http://savannah.gnu.org/cgi-bin/viewcvs/config/config/config.guess?rev=HEAD&content-type=text/plain'
$ wget 'http://savannah.gnu.org/cgi-bin/viewcvs/config/config/config.sub?rev=HEAD&content-type=text/plain'
Less recent versions are also contained in the GNU automake and
GNU libtool packages.
